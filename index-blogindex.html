wanderlust, quenched: california dreamin'

Took a little weeklong trip recently to Northern California after over a year (over a year!) of no vacation...and it did not disappoint. Indeed, our jaunt to San Francisco to visit some friends and then to Big Sur country to do a bit of glamping at Treebones Resort was quite chilled out and pretty restorative for our beat up, tired New York souls. Days were spent driving up and down the indescribably gorgeous Pacific Coast Highway, and nights were spent falling asleep in our Sibley tent to the sounds of seals and the Pacific Ocean directly over the cliff's edge, below.
