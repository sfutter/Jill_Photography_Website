"winged victory" by charlotte noruzi

Human anatomy, fiber art, and ethereally poignant beauty came together in this shoot for fiber artist Charlotte Noruzi. We spent a late morning shooting her breast anatomy piece, "Winged Victory", in her sun-flooded studio space with Charlotte's friend Patti--a breast cancer survivor. Thanks for having me, Charlotte! It was an honor (and so much fun).
